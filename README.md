# Niseko Vacation Vibes

Viral marketing project ideas - brainstormed in Niseko, Japan on NYE 2024.

**Goal:** Create something beautiful, shareable, and attention-grabbing that A16Z might actually fund.

**Constraints:**
- Low infrastructure cost (free tiers, pay-per-use AI)
- Highly shareable output (cards, images people want to post)
- Quick to vibe code
- New Year timing

---

## Ideas

### 1. 2025 in Kanji ‚≠ê (Top Pick)

User enters their 2025 intentions/vibe, AI generates a custom kanji character + meaning representing their year. Beautiful brush calligraphy output on washi paper texture.

**Why viral:**
- Perfect NYE timing
- Highly shareable cards
- Cultural novelty
- "What's your kanji?" is a natural share prompt

**Tech stack:**
- Next.js on Vercel (free tier)
- Claude/GPT for kanji generation + meaning
- Canvas or SVG for calligraphy rendering
- OG image generation for social cards

**A16Z angle:** "AI meets cultural preservation"

---

### 2. The Onsen Oracle

Japanese hot spring fortune teller for 2025. Mystical UI with steam animations, floating fortune cards.

**Why viral:**
- Fortune tellers always pop off around NYE
- Beautiful aesthetic
- "Soak in your destiny" is memorable

**Tech stack:**
- Static site with animations (Framer Motion)
- AI-generated fortunes
- Shareable fortune cards

---

### 3. Hatsuhinode Generator (First Sunrise 2025)

Based on Japanese tradition of watching the first sunrise of the year (hatsuhinode). AI generates personalized sunrise artwork based on your 2025 word/intention.

**Why viral:**
- Culturally significant tradition
- Perfect timing (literally NYE)
- AI art is inherently shareable
- Beautiful, unique outputs

**Tech stack:**
- Next.js
- AI image generation (DALL-E, Midjourney API, or Stable Diffusion)
- Higher cost due to image gen, but could rate limit

---

### 4. Powder Promise

Beautiful digital "commitment contract" for 2025. Japanese aesthetic design, shareable cards.

**Why viral:**
- Accountability is trending
- "Fresh powder, fresh start" resonates
- Niseko connection

**Optional web3 angle:**
- Mint commitment as on-chain attestation
- A16Z loves crypto infra plays

**Tech stack:**
- Next.js
- Beautiful PDF/image generation
- Optional: Base/Ethereum for attestations

---

### 5. Ski Haiku

Enter your vibe, get an AI-generated haiku with beautiful Japanese typography. Snow falling animation, mountain backgrounds.

**Why viral:**
- Dead simple concept
- Extremely shareable
- Haikus are inherently social-media-sized

**Tech stack:**
- Minimal - could be a single page
- AI for haiku generation
- CSS animations for snow
